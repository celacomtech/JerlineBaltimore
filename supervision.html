Clinical Supervision

I offer clinical supervision to Master Level Social Workers (MSW) in the State of Florida. Clinical supervision is provided in group and individual setting.

COVID-19 Alert: Providing Online Supervision Only Online Supervision, Don’t Stress It, I got you! My priority is the safety of all us including our community. Because of this, I am primarily providing online supervision session during the mandated period of social distancing. I have been providing therapy online for years, so I am confident in the ability to do the same for your clinical supervision and ensure the quality of service and connection that you are looking for. In fact, many people find this method to be more convenient and like it better!
