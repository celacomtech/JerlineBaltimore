Starting now, you can begin a brand new journey toward a more positive path for yourself – one that’s filled with growth, well being, acceptance, and empowerment.

I’m Jerline Baltimore, a bilingual English/Spanish licensed therapist and risk management hospital director, graduated from the Florida International University School of Social Work, and a board-certified licensed therapist.
I’m also a member of the National Association of Social Workers, Florida Society of Healthcare Risk Management & Patient Safety, and the American Society for Healthcare Risk Management. But despite my titles and professional recognitions, what I really am is an advocate for change. I work every day to improve the mental and physical health of the under served community of Miami-Dade, this includes clients with substance abuse issues, eating disorders, ACOA’s, depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, trauma, grief and loss, among many others. For years, I have been working as a community hospital medical social worker, closely assisting low-income and under insured individuals and families. My journey started back when I was nine years old, and began feeling connected to something greater than myself. Since I was a child, my grandmother has always been my driving force and a very positive influence, pushing me to become my best version, and be authentic. As a woman who had to be both mother and father to her children, my aim has always been to plan wisely and take action. I listen to people deeply, understand their dilemmas, and help them overcome their challenges more easily. Whenever I see a problem, I simply help people fix it. That’s how I developed such a passion for my professional practice over the years. But in the end, what I do is facilitate growth and health for those that can benefit from my healing energy. I have made that my personal mission for the last 20 years, because I believe there is a mind, body, and spirit connection, and healing takes place on all of those three levels. That’s why my work is a joint effort with my clients, and we work shoulder to shoulder, with dignity and integrity, in an environment where’s there’s no judgment, only understanding.
